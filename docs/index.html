Privacy Policy

Daniel the Plant Whisperer (‏"הלוחש לצמחים"‎) — Last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the messaging assistant used by the Daniel the Plant Whisperer Facebook Page and Instagram account ("we", "us") handles information when you send us a direct message. By messaging our Page or Instagram account, you agree to the practices described below.

What this service does

We use a private assistant to help us reply to the plant-care questions people send us by direct message on Instagram and Facebook Messenger. The assistant drafts a suggested reply, which a human reviews and approves before anything is sent. We do this only to answer your message faster and more helpfully.

Information we process

  • Message content — the text (and description of any photo) you send us in a direct message.
  • Your public profile basics — your name and the message/account identifier that the Instagram and Facebook platforms provide so we can reply to the right conversation.

We do not collect your password, payment details, contact list, or any information beyond what you send us in the conversation.

How we use it

  • To read your message and draft a suggested reply.
  • To let a human review, edit, and approve that reply before it is sent back to you.
  • To avoid answering the same message twice.

We do not use your messages for advertising, and we never sell your information.

Service providers

To operate this assistant we rely on a small number of trusted providers:

  • Meta Platforms (Instagram & Facebook Messenger) — the platforms your message is sent through.
  • Anthropic — the message text is sent to the Claude API to draft a suggested reply. Anthropic processes it to generate the draft and does not use it to train its models.
  • Railway — secure cloud hosting where the assistant runs and where messages are stored.

Data retention

Messages are stored only as long as needed to manage and respond to conversations, and to prevent duplicate replies. You can ask us to delete your message data at any time using the contact details below, and we will do so promptly.

Your choices

  • You can stop the processing at any time by no longer messaging us.
  • You can request access to, or deletion of, the message data we hold about you.

Children

This service is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent change.

Contact
